[b]⚡ ABSOLUTE MOVING AVERAGE SYSTEM (ARMAS)[/b]

ARMAS is the backbone of the indicator. It constructs a dynamic price pipe using EWMA (Exponentially Weighted Moving Average) of highs and lows, forming upper and lower bands that hug price action and adapt naturally to market structure. The result is a clean, responsive channel that tells you at a glance whether price is trending, ranging, or overextended — without the lag of traditional moving averages.

[list]
[*] EWMA-based pipe built from highs and lows — not ATR, not static multipliers
[*] Upper and lower bands track price structure dynamically
[*] Multi-timeframe trend bias derived from the EWMA channel
[*] Color-coded channels: bullish (blue/green), bearish (red), ranging (neutral)
[*] Identifies extended price moves before mean reversion
[/list]

[b]🔍 OPEN INTEREST SIGNAL MATRIX[/b]

Most indicators tell you what price did. The OI Matrix tells you [i]why[/i]. By cross-referencing price action with open interest changes, it classifies current market conditions into 7 distinct states — so you always know who is actually moving price.

[list]
[*] [b]New Longs[/b] — Price up + OI up → genuine buyers entering the market
[*] [b]Short Cover[/b] — Price up + OI down → shorts being forced out
[*] [b]Weak Rally[/b] — Price up + OI flat → low conviction, fade candidate
[*] [b]New Shorts[/b] — Price down + OI up → genuine sellers entering the market
[*] [b]Long Liquidation[/b] — Price down + OI down → longs being stopped out
[*] [b]Liq Bounce[/b] — Price down + OI flat → weak selling pressure
[*] [b]Mixed[/b] — No clear signal → stay patient
[/list]
