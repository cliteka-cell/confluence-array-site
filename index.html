ARMAS

Absolute Moving Average System — an EWMA-based pipe built from highs and lows that hugs price structure dynamically. Instantly see whether price is trending, ranging, or overextended.

Multi-Timeframe VWAP

Daily, Weekly, and Monthly VWAP with standard deviation bands. Anchored to the correct session for each instrument — crypto and CME futures handled separately.

Fibonacci Pivots

Previous week and previous day high/low levels with Fibonacci retracements automatically plotted. Key institutional reference levels, always visible.

OI Signal Matrix

Classifies market conditions into 7 states using Open Interest divergence — New Longs, Short Cover, Long Liquidation, and more. Know who is actually moving price.

Session & Sub-Session Tracker

London, New York, and Asia sessions highlighted automatically. The live dashboard goes deeper — showing the active sub-session (Opening Bell, Rev. Window, Ldn/NY Overlap, Power Hour, and more), a countdown to its end, and the next sub-session coming up.

Intelligent Bar Coloring

Two modes: color bars by trend direction or by OI signal. ADX-based opacity means color intensity reflects conviction — faded in choppy markets, vivid in strong trends.

OI Signal Matrix

Know Who Is Moving Price

Most indicators tell you what price did.
The OI Matrix tells you why.

New Longs

Price up + OI up — genuine buyers entering

Short Cover

Price up + OI down — shorts being forced out

Weak Rally

Price up + OI flat — low conviction move

New Shorts

Price down + OI up — genuine sellers entering

Long Liq

Price down + OI down — longs being stopped out

Liq Bounce

Price down + OI flat — weak selling pressure

Mixed

No clear signal — stay patient
