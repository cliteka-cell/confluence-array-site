ARMAS

Absolute Moving Average System - an EWMA-based pipe built from highs and lows that hugs price structure dynamically. Instantly see whether price is trending, ranging, or overextended.

Multi-Timeframe VWAP

Daily, Weekly, and Monthly VWAP with standard deviation bands. Anchored to the correct session for each instrument - crypto and CME futures handled separately.

Confluence Zones

Previous week and day levels, Fibonacci pockets, value areas and naked POCs are clustered and scored, so the levels where the most signals stack are obvious at a glance.

OI Signal Matrix

Classifies market conditions into 7 states using Open Interest divergence - New Longs, Short Cover, Long Liquidation, and more. Know who is actually moving price.

Session & Sub-Session Tracker

London, New York, and Asia sessions highlighted automatically. The live dashboard drills deeper, showing the active sub-session, a countdown to the end of the current window, and a preview of the next. Always know where you are in the trading day.

Intelligent Bar Coloring

Two modes: color bars by trend direction or by OI signal. ADX-based opacity means color intensity reflects conviction - faded in choppy markets, vivid in strong trends.
